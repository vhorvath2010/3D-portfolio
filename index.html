About Me

I am Vincent, a computer science student at Georgia Tech focusing on intelligence, modeling and simulation, and astrophysics. I am President of the Google Developer Student Club at GT and am wrapping up my summer's work as a Digital Innovation intern for Westinghouse Electric Company where I modernized the workflow of procedures and developed an augmented reality application for steam generator training.


I am joining the Computational Cosmology Group headed by Dr. John Wise this fall 2022 to assist with simulations of the early universe. In the fall of 2021 I worked under doctoral student Anh Thai in Rehg Lab and created a distance-based loss function for a machine learning model. The model creates 3D shapes represented by signed distance fields from single 2D images. I have also worked with the University of Pennsylvania's aviary in the summer of 2021 to use spectrogram analysis and RNN technology to identify cowbird songs in audio data.
